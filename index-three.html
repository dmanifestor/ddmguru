My Working Process, Your Dream Website In Just Few Steps

Creating a website shouldn’t feel overwhelming. I’ve streamlined my process to turn your vision into a fully functional, beautiful website in just a few clear, collaborative steps. Here's how we make your dream website a reality:

concept

Every great website starts with a clear idea. This phase is all about understanding you—your brand, goals, audience, and what success looks like. We’ll discuss:

  • Your vision and objectives
  • Core features and functionality
  • Inspiration sites or design references
  • Content planning and structure

    • The outcome? A solid foundation and a shared vision that guides the rest of the project.

    design

    Now it's time to bring ideas to life. I’ll craft a visual experience that’s not just beautiful, but intuitive and user-friendly. In this phase, I focus on:

  • Wireframes and layout structure
  • Branding (colors, fonts, logos)
  • Responsive UI design (mobile & desktop)
  • User experience (UX) optimization

    • You'll receive design previews to review and approve—ensuring the look and feel matches your expectations before moving to development.

    development

    With the design approved, I’ll dive into coding your site using clean, modern, and scalable practices. This step includes:

  • Front-end and back-end development
  • CMS integration (WordPress, Webflow, or custom)
  • Performance and SEO optimization
  • Testing across devices and browsers

    • Once everything is tested and polished, we’ll go live—and I’ll provide training or documentation so you feel confident managing your new site.
